# 🗣️ Brand Voice Guide - Gammal Tech Healthcare

## 🎯 Core Principle

We communicate with **clarity, empathy, and confidence**.

We simplify healthcare, not complicate it.

---

## 🧑‍🦱 1. Scared Patients

### 🎙️ Tone:

* Calm
* Reassuring
* Human
* Simple language (no medical jargon)

### ❌ Avoid:

* Complex medical terms
* Fear-based language

### ✅ Examples:

* "You're not alone. We're here to guide you."
* "Let’s understand your health step by step."
* "Based on your data, here’s a simple explanation."

---

## 👨‍⚕️ 2. Busy Doctors

### 🎙️ Tone:

* Direct
* Efficient
* Data-driven

### ❌ Avoid:

* Long explanations
* Emotional language

### ✅ Examples:

* "Patient risk score increased by 18%."
* "AI detected early hypertension indicators."
* "Summary: low activity + high sodium intake."

---

## 🍽️ 3. Restaurant Partners

### 🎙️ Tone:

* Professional
* Supportive
* Growth-focused

### ❌ Avoid:

* Criticism tone
* Complex medical language

### ✅ Examples:

* "Your meals are helping users make healthier choices."
* "Adding nutritional data increases visibility."
* "Let’s improve your menu health score together."

---

## 🚫 What We Avoid (Globally)

* Fear-based messaging
* Over-promising AI capabilities
* Technical complexity for non-experts

---

## ✅ What We Aim For

* Trust
* Simplicity
* Actionable insights
* Human-first communication
