# 🏥 Gammal Tech Healthcare - Brand Identity

## 🎯 Brand Goal

The goal of this brand identity is to create a healthcare platform that feels **safe, trustworthy, and simple**.

We focus on:

* Trust
* Safety
* Simplicity
* Human-centered AI

---

## 🧠 Logo Concept

The logo combines:

* ➕ **Medical Cross** → Healthcare & medical services
* 💓 **Pulse Line (ECG)** → Life & real-time monitoring
* 🤖 **Modern Typography** → Technology & AI

The glow effect represents a **soft tech presence** — not aggressive or overwhelming.

## 🎨 Color Palette

### 🔵 Primary Colors

* **Deep Blue**

  * HEX: `#0A1F44`
  * Meaning: Trust, security, professionalism

* **Cyan / Light Blue**

  * HEX: `#22D3EE`
  * Meaning: Technology, clarity, intelligence

---

### 🟣 Secondary Colors

* **Soft Purple**

  * HEX: `#8B5CF6`
  * Meaning: Innovation, AI, futuristic thinking

---

### ⚪ Neutral Colors

* **Light Gray / White**

  * HEX: `#F9FAFB`
  * Meaning: Clean UI, readability

---

## 💬 Emotional Impact

This design aims to make users feel:

* 🛡️ Safe sharing their health data
* 💙 Calm and reassured
* 🤖 Comfortable with AI assistance
* 🧑‍⚕️ Confident in medical insights

---

## 🧪 Design Style

* Minimal
* Clean
* Tech-enabled but human-first
* No flashy or aggressive visuals
